Flash

Flash is an individual that has the ability to travel faster then the speed of light. He uses this ability to help fight crime. Many people argue that Flash's super hero ability is not very great. However, he has proven himself worthy of being called a super hero time and time again.

Flash first appeared to the world in various Flash Comics
